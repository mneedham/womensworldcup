FIFA Women's World Cup Trophy Begins French Tour

Tuesday, 14 May 2019

Following its whirlwind journey taking in each of the 24 countries participating in the upcoming FIFA Women’s World Cup France 2019™, the FIFA Women’s World Cup Trophy is back in the host country. The most coveted prize in women’s football began its national tour of France in Paris. As excitement builds ahead of the tournament, fans throughout France have the opportunity to get up-close, as the trophy will visit a number of locations including stadiums, local schools and city councils, in each of the nine host cities. SNCF, a National Supporter of the tournament, will showcase the trophy on its trains as it is transported between cities.
